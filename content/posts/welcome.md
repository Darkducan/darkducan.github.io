This is the first post on **ui/ux.expert** — a monthly, image-heavy look at how
interfaces are actually built.

## What to expect

Each month, one article that takes a real interface apart: the grid underneath it,
the spacing decisions, the small interactions that make it feel considered instead
of assembled.

## How to write the next one

Go to `/admin` on this site, log in, click **New Post**, write, drag in your
screenshots, and publish. This post is just a placeholder — replace it with your
first real one whenever you're ready.
